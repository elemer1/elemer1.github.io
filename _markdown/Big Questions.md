- How does modern theoretical physics understand the universe?
  现代理论物理学是如何理解宇宙的？
- What are the origins of life and complex structures?
  生命和复杂结构的源头是什么？
- How did modern mathematics become so abstract, and what is it ultimately pursuing?
  现代数学是如何变得如此抽象的，它最终追求的是什么？
- What are intelligence and consciousness, and how can we mathematically characterize the power and limits of intelligence and modern AI?
  什么是智能和意识，我们如何从数学上表征智能和现代人工智能的能力和局限？
- What major historical milestones paved the way for achieving artificial superintelligence?
  实现人工超级智能的重大历史里程碑有哪些？
- How close are we to achieving abundant energy, and what are the major challenges?
  我们离实现充足能源还有多远，主要面临哪些挑战？
- How close are we to curing all diseases, and what are the major challenges?
  我们离治愈所有疾病还有多远，主要面临哪些挑战？
- How close are we to eliminating global poverty (not extreme poverty), and what are the major challenges?
  我们离消除全球贫困（非极端贫困），主要面临哪些挑战？
- What is the next major technological platform shift, and how will it happen?
  下一个主要的科技平台转变是什么，它将如何发生？
- How does money function and shape the modern economy?
  货币如何运作并塑造现代经济？
- How have social and economic structures adapted to each industrial revolution?
  社会和经济结构如何适应每一次工业革命？
- Is the universe fundamentally computational, and what are the absolute physical limits of information processing? 宇宙的本质是计算吗？信息处理的绝对物理极限是什么？
- How do we mathematically model and navigate non-ergodic environments and extreme tail risks in societal evolution?
  我们如何从数学上对社会演进中非各态历经环境和极端尾部风险进行建模与导航？
- How will the "Social Contract" and legal jurisprudence adapt to non-human autonomous agents and algorithmic governance? 
  “社会契约”和法律法理学将如何适应非人类自治代理和算法治理？
- Is biological aging an evolutionary program that can be reverse-engineered, or an inevitable thermodynamic consequence? 
  生物衰老是一种可以被逆向工程的进化程序，还是不可避免的热力学结果？
- How will capital allocation mechanisms evolve when algorithmic agents replace human actors as the primary market participants? 
  当算法代理取代人类成为主要市场参与者时，资本配置机制将如何演变？
